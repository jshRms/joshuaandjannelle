Our Story

Our journey began in 2009 when we were just high school classmates, two young souls finding our way in the world. As the years passed, life led us down different paths, placing us in separate sections and eventually taking us in different directions. Fate, however, had other plans.

In February 2019, our paths crossed again, rekindling a bond that had been waiting to bloom. From that moment on, we shared everything—laughter, deep conversations, personal struggles, and even the occasional arguments. Somewhere along the way, Joshua realized his heart had found its home, and for five months, he patiently courted Jannelle Alyssah, hoping she would see in him what he saw in her. Then, on July 6, 2019, she said yes.

six years, we have walked hand in hand through life's highs and lows. Our story is one of resilience, growth, and unwavering love—a testament to the power of two hearts determined to stay together.

The Proposal

After six years of pure love, honesty, and unwavering commitment, Joshua knew it was time to take the next step—a step that would define the rest of their lives. In August 2024, he made the most thrilling and nerve-wracking decision of his life. Despite the lingering fear of rejection, he pushed past his doubts, driven by the love he held for Jannelle Alyssah.

With a heart pounding in both excitement and anticipation, he got down on one knee, his eyes filled with sincerity and hope. Time seemed to stand still as he asked the most important question he had ever spoken—asking Jannelle Alyssah to be his forever.

For a moment, the world faded into silence. The air was electric with emotion, a mix of excitement, joy, and an indescribable feeling neither of them had ever known before. YES! I DO!

With those words, their love story took a new turn—a promise sealed with pure happiness, a journey towards forever.
